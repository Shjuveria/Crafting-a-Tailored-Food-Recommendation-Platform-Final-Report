Roasted Cauliflower, Broccoli and Golden Beets

Cauliflower, broccoli and beets are roasted together to create a savory treat. The combination is loaded with vitamins, including A, C and K. And research shows that when you eat colors from the rainbow in fruits and vegetables, your body gets a big boost of antioxidants, which help stave off diseases and cellular damage.
